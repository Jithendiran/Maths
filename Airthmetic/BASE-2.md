2.   
```
1 + 1 = 10
place value
            4   2   1
carry:          1
                -------    
                    1 
                    1
                --------
                 1  0

    Decimal
    1 + 1 = 2, 2 => 10

```   

In binary we have only two symbols which is 0 and 1, when the place value reaches 1, for nect digit we will form a group of 2, 1 + 1 = 10 whcih mean 1 group of 2's and 0 extras
